    About Us

    Unity App

    Unity was developed by Amani Kibet Kesir & Rick, Moi University students passionate about tech and global communication. Unity was built to help people worldwide make authentic connections — easily, privately, and totally free.

    Our mission: to create a space where anyone on the globe can chat, discover, and interact — no paywalls, no fuss. Connection should be simple, fun, and accessible to everyone.

    How we built Unity: We gathered feedback and ideas from students across Moi University’s colleges to understand what users truly want from a chat platform — then turned those insights into real features you see today.

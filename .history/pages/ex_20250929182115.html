Unityy Privacy Policy

Unityy is built to connect everyone globally. We respect your privacy and protect your data with end-to-end encryption (E2EE) and self-encryption on your device.

What We Collect

  • Account info: username, email, profile.
  • Device & connection info (for security).
  • Message metadata (not content) for routing.

How We Use It

To deliver messages, improve the app, and prevent abuse. Your messages remain private if E2EE is on.

Sharing & Safety

We never sell your data. Only minimal info goes to service providers or legal authorities if required.

Your Control

Enable/disable E2EE, edit your profile, or delete your account anytime.

For questions, contact: privacy@unityy.example
